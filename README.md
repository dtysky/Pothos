# Pothos
A private literary advisor.

Named after the Greek word `Pothos`: not simple desire, but longing for what is absent, the ache of reaching for what is always just out of reach. If `Eros` is love and `Himeros` is the thrill of what stands before you, `Pothos` is the hand still extended toward what is not here.

## Workspace

- `.pothos/` — rules, skills, and durable knowledge for the advisor itself.
- `projects/` — large creative projects with their own design, research, notes, scripts, and shots.
- `research/` — standalone one-off research reports and topic dossiers that do not yet belong to a large project.
- `notes/` — general consultation notes and discussion archives.
